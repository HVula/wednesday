Nature walk

The term “natural feature” means a feature that is created through the action of

  • Physical
  • Geological
  • Biological, and
  • Chemical

processes over time

Natural features are unmodified components of landscapes, such as

  1. Mountains
  2. Rivers
  3. Wetlands
  4. Hill country
  5. Plains
  6. Coastlines
  7. Rocky outcrops and
  8. Bluffs/cliffs
